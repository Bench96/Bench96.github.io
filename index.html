Silnex Enterprise

Company Profile

Silenx Enterprises was founded in 2017 with the main purpose of expanding into the feasibility study space, with its main purpose of expanding into larger parts of Africa and furthering the business sphere.

Each project is managed by our team of highly experienced personnel, incorporating the services of various professionals in the engineering, prospecting, quantity surveying and other related fields, with whom we have established an excellent working relationship.

With continued focus on high growth emerging markets, Silenx wants to cement its position as a market leader of commercial, retail and residential development in various regions in and around the SADC region.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to become the premier infrastructure development company in Africa

Our mission is to be the partner of choice, provide value-added infrastructure and mining solutions, surpassing customer expectations. And to create wealth and employment by developing not only infrastructure but entire communities in the process.

Honesty and Integrity

Uncompromising integrity, honesty and fairness will be at the core of everything Silenx Enterprises does. Silenx is a company that is private by design and its success is based on recommendations of people moved by Silenx ability to develop and implement infrastructure projects. Hence every Silenx employee is a Brand Ambassador of the company.

Our Passion

Pioneering Africa

With Africa’s emergence as an economic block of global interest, Silenx remains true to its pioneering roots in developing infrastructure and sustainable energy projects on the Continent.

Local Developments

Whilst we have an exciting future in Africa, we remain highly focused on our local portfolio of servicing existing infrastructure and mining operations.
