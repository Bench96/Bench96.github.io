N'WA-VANGANI PRIMARY SCHOOL

We as the educators, non-educators and the governing body of Nwa-Vangani Primary School are committed to the pursuit of excellence.

Our School

We offer primary education from grade R to grade 7. Our students enjoy themselves, not only academically, but also through different activities that are available to them. These include a number of sporting activities such as soccer, netball, cricket and athletics, as well as other interesting cultural activities such as drum majorettes. Student life at N'wa-Vangani Primary School is highly balanced and is still constantly being improved through the sponsorships and through contributions by our hard working staff members. ".... We as the students at N'wa-Vangani primary school are fervent and determined to learn. We also believe that education is the best key to success, so with the help of our great teachers we know that we will achieve great things...."

Vision

We as the Educators, Non-educators and the Governing Body of Nwa-Vangani Primary School are committed to the pursuit of excellence. We believe in quality and progressive education that aims at the development of responsible young people who are fully equipped for success - academically, spiritually, and morally. Emphasis is placed on the individuality of each learner to develop special talents and abilities, whilst a strongly disciplined environment and firm Christian values provide security and guidance.

Mission Statement

We serve the learners and the community through providing quality education on skills training and whole person development by giving attention to their intellectual, emotional, social and spiritual needs. We further provide for adequate human and physical resources and we develop human dignity.
